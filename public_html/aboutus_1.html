Community Involvement

  • Shay Oil supports many charities and fundraising events in Southwestern Arizona.
  • We have supported the annual Midnight at the Oasis (MATO) car show for many years.
  • We arranged for Mario Andretti to appear at the MATO car show in 2008.
  • We also support local service clubs (such as Rotary ) in Yuma.
  • We support our local community with fundraising events through out the year.
  • We buy goods and services from a number of local companies.
  • We paid for traffic lights at 8th Street and Avenue C in Yuma, and at Main St. and Riggles Ave. in Quartzsite.
  • Many of our stores recycle cardboard, use drip irrigation in their landscaping, use
    low-energy LED lights in their canopies, or all three. We recycle our used laser printer cartridges.
